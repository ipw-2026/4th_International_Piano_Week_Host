Schedule of Events

Sun, July 26

Arrival & Welcome

Arrival of all International Piano Week (IPW) participants to the campus. Check-in and orientation.

Mon, July 27

Opening Concert

Day begins with lessons and masterclasses. The evening features the Guest Artist Opening Concert to inaugurate the week.

Tue, July 28

Frederick Piano Collection Tour

A special tour in the morning/early afternoon. The Fredericks will present their historical instrument collection, and students will have the unique opportunity to try the pianos. Evening: Faculty Concert.

Wed, July 29

Field Trip & Guest Masterclass

A designated Field Trip day. The afternoon includes a special masterclass with the Guest Artist.

Thu, July 30

Masterclasses & Concert

Full day of intensive lessons and masterclasses with IPW faculty and guest artists. Evening performance (TBA).

Fri, July 31

Participant Gala Concert

The culmination of the week's work. A showcase concert featuring performances by the IPW participants.

Sat, Aug 1

Departure

All participants depart campus by 12:00 noon.
